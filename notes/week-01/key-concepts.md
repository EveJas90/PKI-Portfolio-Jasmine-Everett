# Week 01 — Key Concepts

## PKI Explanation (3–5 Sentences)

Write a clear explanation of what PKI is in your own words.

Focus on:
- The problem PKI solves
- The role of keys
- The role of certificates
- How trust is established

PKI solves the problem of digital trust by providing identites a simpler way of identifying themselves. PKI uses key pairs, one public and one private key, the public key encrypts the data and the private key keeps that data secure by being available to the owner of the private key. Each key has a certificate that acts as an ID to help identify who the keys belong to. Trust is estabished by an trusted authority that verfies the certificates and keys match the identity that is trying to authenticate.
